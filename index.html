Overfishing is the Problem!

Why is overfishing a worldwide issue, and how can we help fix it?

Overfishing is a major issue and a driving factor in the decline of ocean wildlife.

Fishing by itslef is not bad by itslef, but it becomes an issue when fish are removed from the environment faster than it can be replenished.

This is called OVERFISHING, and it causes major environmental damage.

  • Fishing not only destroys ecosystems, but:
  • Fishing is one of the most lucrative trades in the world, and lots of businesses rely on this as their livelihood.
  • Many places live off of fishing as their main food source or income.
