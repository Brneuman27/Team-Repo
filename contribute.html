How to Combat Overfishing.

There are lots of ways to combat overfishing, some easier than others. They include:

  • Using sustainable fishing techniques to decrease the amount of accidental non-target catches.
  • Establishing protected areas in the ocean where fishing is prohibited.
  • Implementing seasonal or size restrictions on the fish.
  • Educating people about sustainable fishing techniques and endangered species

These are all major stepping stones to decreasing overfishing in the world.

Many of these things have already been implemented, but not enforced as much as it should be.
